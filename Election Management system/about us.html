About Us

Welcome to the official Government Electoral Voting System portal.

This platform is authorized and maintained by the Election Commission of India under the guidance of the Chief Election Commissioner. Our goal is to ensure a transparent, secure, and convenient voting experience for all eligible citizens of India.

To participate in the electoral process:

- Please complete your registration through the Signup page with accurate details.
- Once registered, use the Login page to access your constituency candidates.
- You may then select your preferred candidate and submit your vote securely.
